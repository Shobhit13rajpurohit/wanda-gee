The Global Advantage

Why Establish a GCC in India?

India has evolved from a cost-arbitrage back-office destination to a global hub for innovation, digital transformation, and high-value capability centers. Today, India hosts over 1,500 Global Capability Centers (GCCs), employing over 1.3 million professionals and generating nearly $34 billion in revenue. The country offers an unmatched ecosystem of engineering talent, data scientists, and domain experts.

For multinational corporations, setting up a GCC in India is no longer just about operational efficiency—it is a strategic imperative. By leveraging India's demographic dividend and robust digital infrastructure, companies can accelerate product development, enhance customer experiences, and drive global business resilience. Syncronisers facilitates this journey by providing a risk-free, compliant, and accelerated entry pathway.
