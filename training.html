French Language Training for Students

At Wanda & GEE, we offer a comprehensive French language program designed for students in Jaipur. Our courses are structured to take you from beginner to advanced levels, equipping you with the skills to communicate fluently and confidently.

Our training center in Jaipur provides an immersive learning environment with certified instructors and interactive sessions. Join us to explore French culture and open up a world of new opportunities.

Our Curriculum

What You Will Learn

Our curriculum is aligned with the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR). We cover all aspects of the language, from grammar and vocabulary to conversation and cultural nuances.

Courses range from A1 (Beginner) to B2 (Upper Intermediate), focusing on practical communication skills for academic, professional, and personal growth. Interactive exercises, real-life scenarios, and multimedia resources make learning engaging and effective.

Frequently Asked Questions

What levels of French courses are available?
We offer courses for all levels, from A1 (Beginner) to B2 (Upper Intermediate), catering to both new learners and those looking to advance their skills.

Who are the instructors?
Our instructors are certified language experts with extensive experience in teaching French. They provide personalized guidance to help you achieve your learning goals.

What is the duration of the course?
The duration for each level is typically three months, with flexible batch timings to accommodate student schedules. Contact us for more details.
