Become an Entrepreneur

Micro-Franchise Opportunities

Wanda & GEE offers low-investment micro-franchise opportunities for students who want to become entrepreneurs. As a student franchisee, you will be responsible for managing our services in your college or locality, giving you hands-on experience in business management and marketing.

This is a unique opportunity to earn while you learn and build a valuable network. Contact us to learn more about our student franchise program in Jaipur.
